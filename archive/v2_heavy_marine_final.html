Provenance & Utility

We occupy the space between high-performance technical research and the raw, unyielding utility of the modern dock worker. This isn't just clothing; it is inherited equipment.

Our materials are harvested from retired racing sails across the Genoa Docks. Dacron, Kevlar, and Mylar hulls that have endured the brutality of the open sea are reclaimed and re-engineered. No thread. Just mechanical origami and steel rivets.

Limited & Traceable

Every piece is a unique node in a closed logistics chain. Integrated NTAG213 NFC Chips embedded in heat-sealed rubber manifests provide a Digital Product Passport.

Scan to verify the unit's origin—from the Genoa Source to the Liverpool Muscle to the final Amsterdam Terminal assembly. Every item tells a story of survival and structural integrity.
