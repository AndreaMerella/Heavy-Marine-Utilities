The Structural
Witness.

Before it was your gear, it was a witness to the gale. These hulls of Dacron and Kevlar have spent decades under crushing tension, absorbing the salt of the Mediterranean and the bite of the North Sea. They are retired veterans of the racing circuit, carrying the scars of every tack and jibe. We don't discard this history; we harvest it.

We honor the legacy of the silent laborers—the Camalli of Genova, the Mersey Stevedores, the Havenarbeiders. No thread. Just industrial origami and steel rivets. We fused that raw necessity with clinical industrial engineering. This is upcycling as a form of resistance. A commitment to an indestructible future rooted in a rugged past.
